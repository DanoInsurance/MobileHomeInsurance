About Us

Finding insurance for your mobile/manufactured home can be difficult. This website has been created to help consumers find insurance coverage for thier home.

  • Every insurance company operates differently. There are underwriting considerations which could affect the final price and eligibility of a policy.
  • At times, there may be multiple carriers willing to write a policy for your home. In such instances you will be provided with quotes from these companies.
  • Not all carriers are willing to write policies in all areas. On this site you can find a list of counties currently open and the base rates of a policy.
